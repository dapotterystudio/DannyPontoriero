Journal entry

The market was louder than expected and more golden than the photographs suggested. Fish scales flashed in shallow trays, men called out over crates of lemons, and every corner smelled briefly of oil before turning into herbs and warm bread again.

The thing that stayed was not one perfect dish but the chain reaction between place and appetite. Chickpea batter in the air made fried panelle feel inevitable. A narrow side street later made dinner feel earned. That is exactly why the site pairs stories with places and recipes instead of treating them as separate content types with separate emotional weight.
