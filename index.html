Danny Pontoriero
Info

Daniel Pontoriero is a designer and ceramic artist exploring the balance between function and sculptural expression. With a Bachelor's in Environmental Design: Sustainable Planning from the University of Colorado Boulder and a Master's in Urban Visioning and Architectural Design from Domus Academy in Milan, his work bridges architecture, design, and craftsmanship.

His ceramics blend traditional wheel throwing with hand building techniques, pushing the boundaries of form and material through playful, determined objects.
